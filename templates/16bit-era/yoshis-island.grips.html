Yoshi's Island

Nintendo threw its players a curve ball in 1995 by giving Mario a break from kidnapped-royalty recovery and instead rewinding our hero's timeline to his days of wearing diapers. Baby Mario made his debut, and we discovered that his first-ever adventure was, yes, with his dino pal Yoshi.

After Luigi is kidnapped by an evil wizard named Kamek, Baby Mario finds himself rescued by a gang of friendly Yoshi...Yoshis...umm, whatever. Yoshi could eat enemies, fire eggs, and transform into a wealth of different vehicles. All of this, combined with some of the best music the series has ever heard, makes Yoshi's Island a complete gem.

  • Console: SNES
  • Director: Takashi Tezuka
