Handhelds

Nintendo's various handhelds have played home to Mario adventures since Super Mario Land hit the original Game Boy in 1989. Since then, Mario has traveled through space, hung out with a Sphinx, popped out of the screen on the 3DS, and handed the reigns off to his weird buddy Wario.

Nintendo's handhelds have also played home to various remakes and ports of classic Mario games. Being able to complete Super Mario World on a single flight, or hop through a remixed version of Mario 64 as different characters have provided some of the best handheld experiences in all of gaming.
