Super Mario Bros. 3

No sooner did the heroes return triumphant from their journey through the dream world than the princess decided to get herself kidnapped a second time.

This time, the Mushroom Kingdom had expanded, now encompassing castles built in deserts, on seashores, and even on a cloud in the sky. It was the grandest, most taxing adventure he'd had yet – but, luckily, he had some new help too.

More plentiful power-ups populated every new place he visited. He could dress up like a frog in order to swim more swiftly. He could even fly – not with wings, but with a multi-functional raccoon tail that could be wagged repeatedly to send him skyward.

  • Console: Nintendo Entertainment System
  • Directors: Shigeru Miyamoto & Takashi Tezuka
