Super Smash Bros., Mario Party, & Mario Sports

Sometimes, Mario needs a break from running right, jumping, and sliding down flagpoles. A guy can only save a princess so many times before he needs a bit of R&R. Luckily, he has plenty of ways to relieve some of that monotony.

Battling against Nintendo's other iconic characters, stretching his legs for a bit of tennis, and partaking in a diabolical suite of mini-games are just a handful of the activities that Mario has participated in over the past 15 years.

The wide array of genres that Mario has dabbled in shows just how versatile the character is. I mean, who else could go from plumber to olympiad in a single day?
