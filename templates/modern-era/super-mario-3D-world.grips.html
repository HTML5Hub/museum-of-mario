Super Mario 3D Land & World

Which brings us to where Mario stands today – poised and prepared for his next 3D platforming adventure, a follow-up to 3D Land the first that will appear on Wii U.

Like Super Mario 64, it will let players explore in any direction they choose. Like Super Mario Bros. 2, it will give them a choice between four different heroes. And like Super Mario Bros. 3, it will introduce all new power-ups for our hero to claim – like the Cat Bell, which will transform him into a claw-scratching, wall-climbing feline.

This fall can't come soon enough.

  • Console: 3DS & Wii U
  • Directors: Koichi Hayashida (Super Mario 3D Land), Koichi Hayashida (Super Mario 3D World)
